
### Mercatec – JSP E-commerce Platform (Second Place Award)

Academic prototype developed for the 32nd National Contest “Premio a los Mejores Prototipos del Nivel Medio Superior” organized by the Instituto Politécnico Nacional (IPN).

The project was awarded **Second Place** in the category *Application for Business*.

Mercatec is an e-commerce web platform designed to digitalize local markets in Mexico, allowing vendors to publish products and expand their commercial reach.
